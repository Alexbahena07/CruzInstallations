Why Choose Cruz

Local Flooring Installers Who Treat Your Home Like Ours

We've spent years installing flooring across the Chicago area, and we know what works in this climate and in these homes. Chicago winters are tough on entryways, basements flood without warning, and busy households need flooring that holds up to kids, pets, and everyday life.

That's why we recommend the right product for your room — not the most expensive one. And why we install it with the careful seam work, tight transitions, and clean finish you'd expect from a top-rated local pro.

  • Honest product recommendations for your space
  • Clean seam work and tight transitions
  • Flooring built to handle Chicago weather and real life
